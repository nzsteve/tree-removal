Why Hire an Arborist in North Rocks?

An experienced arborist specializes in the cultivation, management, and study of trees. Their knowledge extends beyond just tree removal; it encompasses every aspect of tree care that ensures their healthy growth and safety to surrounding structures.

FAQ Section About an Arborist in North Rocks

What determines when I need a professional arborist?

If you notice signs of decay, overgrown branches touching power lines, pest infestations within your trees or even storm damage—these scenarios warrant professional attention from an arborist.

Is there a difference between trimming and pruning?

Yes! While both methods remove branches to promote healthy growth:

  • Trimming generally refers to aesthetic shaping,
  • Pruning focuses on health by removing dead or dying branches.

How often should I contact my arborist?

Typically once per year for routine maintenance works well unless severe weather events occur requiring inspections following storms or other disturbances that could harm your trees better being addressed quickly!

Can I perform tree care myself instead?

While minor tasks may seem manageable:

  • Always err on caution before undertaking significant jobs (eg, heavy lifting involved when cutting large branches),
  • Consulting professionals avoids possible injuries or ultimate regrets over improper care resulting in death!
