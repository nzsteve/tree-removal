FAQs About Arborist Services

Q: How often should I have my trees trimmed?
A: Frequency may vary depending on the species of trees on your property. Generally, annual inspections and light trimming can help maintain the health and aesthetics of most trees.

Q: Is it necessary to hire an arborist for routine tree care?
A: While minor tasks like basic pruning may be manageable for some homeowners, consulting an arborist ensures that professional standards are met when caring for valuable landscape assets.

Q: What should I consider before undertaking a large-scale tree removal project?
A: Factors such as local regulations, potential impact on surrounding vegetation, utility lines, structures, wildlife habitats must be carefully evaluated before scheduling extensive tree removals.
