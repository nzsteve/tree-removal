FAQs about Arborist Services in Stanhope Gardens

  1. How often should I prune my trees?

    • The frequency of pruning depends on the species and their growth rate. It is generally recommended to prune deciduous trees every 3-5 years, while evergreen trees may require less frequent pruning.

  2. Can an arborist save a dying tree?

    • In many cases, an arborist can identify the cause of decline and implement measures to restore a tree's health. However, some severely damaged or diseased trees may be beyond saving and require removal.

  3. Are there any restrictions on removing trees in Stanhope Gardens?

    • Local regulations vary, so it is important to check with your local council before removing any significant trees from your property. Working with an arborist ensures compliance with relevant rules and permits.

  4. Will an arborist clean up after tree removal?

    • Yes, reputable arborists include cleaning up debris as part of their service offerings. They will ensure that your property is left neat and tidy once the job is completed.
