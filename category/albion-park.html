FAQs About Tree Lopping in Wollonggong

1. How often should tree lopping be done?

Tree lopping frequency depends on various factors such as tree species, growth rate, and proximity to structures. Generally, it is recommended to have trees inspected and pruned every 3-5 years to maintain their health and structural integrity.

2. Can I trim my trees on my own?

While minor pruning can be performed by homeowners, it is advisable to hire professional arborists for significant tree lopping or removals. They possess the necessary skills, knowledge, and equipment to carry out these tasks safely and effectively.

3. Is tree lopping harmful to trees?

When conducted correctly by professionals, tree lopping is not harmful. In fact, it promotes healthy growth by eliminating weak or diseased branches that can potentially cause harm.
